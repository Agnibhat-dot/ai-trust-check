About AI Trust Check

Our mission: To become the world's most trusted AI verification platform, empowering millions to verify information with confidence.

Our Mission

Millions now use AI every day, but they often don't know whether to trust an answer, image, website, email, or screenshot.

AI Trust Check exists to solve this problem. We believe everyone deserves access to tools that help them verify information and evaluate the trustworthiness of AI-generated and online content.

We're building the most comprehensive, accessible, and reliable AI verification platform on the internet.

Our Values

  • ✓ Accessibility - Free tools for everyone
  • ✓ Transparency - Clear methodology and limitations
  • ✓ Accuracy - Rigorous analysis and testing
  • ✓ Privacy - Your data is never sold or misused
  • ✓ Trust - We're accountable to our users
