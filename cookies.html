Cookie Policy

Learn how AI Trust Check uses cookies and tracking technologies.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device that contain information about your browsing behavior. They allow websites to remember your preferences and improve your experience.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

Required for basic functionality:

  • Theme Preference: Remembers your dark/light mode choice
  • Session Data: Maintains your session while using tools
  • Security: Protects against security threats

Analytics Cookies (Optional)

Help us understand how you use the service:

  • Google Analytics: Anonymous traffic data (opt-in)
  • Usage Statistics: Popular tools and features
  • Performance Data: Page load times and errors

Advertising Cookies (Optional)

Used for Google AdSense and targeted ads:

  • Google AdSense: Personalized advertising
  • Ad Preferences: Tailored ad content
  • Conversion Tracking: Campaign effectiveness

3. Third-Party Cookies

We use cookies from:

  • Google Analytics: Website analytics
  • Google AdSense: Advertising platform
  • Facebook Pixel: Cross-site analytics (optional)

4. Your Cookie Preferences

You can control cookies through:

  • Your browser's cookie settings
  • Opting out of analytics in our privacy settings
  • Do Not Track (DNT) browser features
  • Google's ads personalization controls

5. Cookie Retention

Cookie duration varies:

  • Session Cookies: Deleted when you close your browser
  • Persistent Cookies: Retained for up to 2 years
  • Analytics: Up to 26 months (Google Analytics default)

6. Disabling Cookies

You can disable cookies in your browser settings:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies
  • Firefox: Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data
  • Edge: Settings → Privacy → Cookies

7. Do Not Track (DNT)

If your browser sends a DNT signal, we respect your choice and limit tracking. However, some features may not work optimally.

8. GDPR & CCPA Compliance

EU (GDPR): Essential cookies are enabled by default. Analytics and advertising require explicit consent.

California (CCPA): You have the right to opt-out of the sale or sharing of your personal information.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this cookie policy periodically. Changes take effect immediately upon posting.
