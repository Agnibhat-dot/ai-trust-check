The Problem

Misinformation spreads faster than truth:

  • ⚠️ Fabricated Headlines - False or misleading article titles
  • ⚠️ False Claims - Unverified or debunked facts presented as news
  • ⚠️ Media Bias - Slanted reporting from unreliable sources
  • ⚠️ Satire Confusion - Comedy sites mistaken for news
  • ⚠️ Clickbait - Sensationalized content designed to mislead

Our Solution

The Fake News Checker analyzes articles using multiple verification layers:

  • ✓ Source Reputation - Check domain credibility history
  • ✓ Fact Analysis - Cross-reference claims with trusted sources
  • ✓ Bias Detection - Identify political or ideological slant
  • ✓ Language Analysis - Flag sensationalized or manipulative language
  • ✓ Credibility Score - Get a clear 0-100 trustworthiness rating
