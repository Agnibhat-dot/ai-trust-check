The Problem

Malicious websites exploit trust:

  • ⚠️ Phishing Sites - Fake sites mimicking legitimate companies
  • ⚠️ Missing SSL - No encryption for data transmission
  • ⚠️ New Domains - Recently registered suspicious sites
  • ⚠️ Poor Reputation - Known for scams or malware
  • ⚠️ Suspicious Indicators - Hidden contact info, privacy abuse

    Frequently Asked Questions

    What's a trust score?

    0-100 rating based on SSL validity, domain age, reputation history, and security records.

    Is HTTPS enough?

    HTTPS means data is encrypted, but doesn't guarantee the site is legitimate. We check much deeper.

    Can I trust new domains?

    New domains (under 6 months) are riskier. Established domains have longer reputation history to verify.

    How do you detect phishing?

    We analyze domain registration, SSL certificates, content, and compare against known phishing patterns.
