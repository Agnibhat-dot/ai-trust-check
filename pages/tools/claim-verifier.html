The Problem

Unverified claims spread misinformation:

  • ⚠️ Partial Truth - Claims mixing fact and fiction
  • ⚠️ Missing Context - True facts presented misleadingly
  • ⚠️ Outdated Info - Old data presented as current
  • ⚠️ Weak Evidence - Claims lacking strong support
  • ⚠️ Complex Statements - Hard to know what to verify

Our Solution

Intelligent claim analysis and verification:

  • ✓ Claim Breakdown - Separate main claim into testable parts
  • ✓ Evidence Identification - Show what needs verification
  • ✓ Trusted Sources - Suggest authoritative sources to check
  • ✓ Confidence Score - Rate verifiability and reliability
  • ✓ Risk Assessment - Identify common misinformation patterns
