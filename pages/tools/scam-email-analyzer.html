The Problem

Email scams cost billions annually:

  • ⚠️ Phishing - Fake emails impersonating legitimate companies
  • ⚠️ Spoofing - Forged sender addresses
  • ⚠️ Urgency Tactics - False deadlines to bypass thinking
  • ⚠️ Malicious Links - URLs leading to scam or malware sites
  • ⚠️ Grammar Errors - Poor language indicating non-professionals

Our Solution

  • ✓ Link Analysis - Check URL reputation and redirects
  • ✓ Content Analysis - Detect urgency and social engineering
  • ✓ Language Check - Identify grammar and spelling patterns

    Frequently Asked Questions

    What's a scam probability?

    0-100% likelihood that the email is phishing, spoofing, or a scam attempt.

    How do you detect phishing?

    We analyze sender headers, domain reputation, links, content patterns, and compare against known phishing signatures.

    What's email spoofing?

    When scammers forge the sender address to impersonate a legitimate company.

    Should I click links?

    Never click links in suspicious emails. Use this tool first, or verify the company separately.
