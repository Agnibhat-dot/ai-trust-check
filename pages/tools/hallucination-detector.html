      Frequently Asked Questions

      What's a hallucination?

      When AI makes up facts that sound plausible but aren't true or verifiable.

      How do you detect them?

      We identify unsupported claims, check references, flag suspicious statistics, and highlight overcconfident language.

      Why do AI hallucinate?

      AI generates text based on patterns, not facts. It can't distinguish between real and plausible-sounding information.

      Should I never trust AI?

      Use AI as a starting point, but verify all claims, especially statistics, citations, and factual information.
