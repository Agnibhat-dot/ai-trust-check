FAQ

How does moderation work?

Our team reviews all submissions within 24 hours. Reports are checked for accuracy and compliance with guidelines before publishing.

Can I edit my report?

Yes, you can edit your report for up to 7 days after submission. After that, contact support.

Will my information be private?

Yes. We don't share your email or personal information. Only approved reports are published.

How is "helpful" determined?

Users can vote on whether reports are helpful. Most helpful reports get featured and ranked higher.
